Rockport Investment Partners

A financial consulting firm that seamlessly integrates valuation, merger and acquisition assistance, and investment banking services.

We support asset managers, investors, owners, buyers, and sellers of private companies and small businesses with clear and meaningful financial analysis and advice, and champion their interests in a transaction.

Valuations

We customize and deliver valuation analyses of businesses and business interests for a variety of purposes including: management planning, M&A/transaction advisory, tax, financial reporting, dispute resolution, and restructuring.

M&A Advising

We advise investors and owners in the acquisition and divestiture of businesses and business interests with services such as: M&A financial due diligence/quality of earnings, financial modeling, fairness opinions, purchase price allocations, and other consulting services.

Investment Banking

We guide privately held companies through each step of a transaction process to complete the sale of their business including: valuation and market analysis, preparation of marketing materials, presentation of the acquisition opportunity to strategic and/or financial buyers, negotiation of terms with potential buyers, and completion of the transaction.
